Built around traditional family values and an uncompromising commitment to integrity, premium service and social responsibility, the Group is renowned for delivering luxury products and services while providing the best possible experience for its customers across a wide range of industries.

Alfardan Jewellery

Established in 1954, Alfardan Jewellery is a business built on the concept of providing the finest quality to meet the needs and tastes of each client.

Alfardan Jewellery has always been a leader in offering prominent pieces of jewellery and statement watches to the people of Qatar. It achieved this by being the first in the region to hold a private Jewellery and Watches Exhibition in 1977, which has become a public annual event since 2003. The world’s finest and most famous jewellery and watch designers and manufacturers participate in the event, which draws many visitors both locally and regionally, and has been recognised by the government for its historical, cultural and economic value and significance.

Alfardan Jewellery has also launched its own line, Noudar, created by Noor Alfardan, the daughter of Ali Hussain Alfardan, President of Alfardan Jewellery. Specialising in elegant and contemporary jewellery with a traditional twist, Noudar’s inspiration is drawn from the depth and extent of the family’s involvement in jewellery spread back across generations.

Alfardan Jewellery built on its reputation in Qatar by establishing a strong presence in Saudi Arabia in 1992. With branches in Riyadh, Jeddah and Al Khobar, it provides high quality watches and jewellery to the local discerning customers.

Alfardan Properties

Established in 1993 and initially created to manage the family’s own properties, Alfardan Properties has blossomed into one of Qatar’s best luxury real estate developers. Servicing both residential and commercial clientele, Alfardan Properties is always striving to provide functional luxury accompanied with lifestyle features for its tenants.

Its commitment is to enrich tenants’ quality of life, whether in a residential or commercial space. Served by well-trained professionals, the properties are strategically located, exquisitely designed, and fully secure. Luxurious end-to-end living solutions create an outstanding environment, allowing tenants to experience Alfardan Living.

Alfardan Properties is certified with ISO 9001:2015 certification for its Quality Management System (QMS). This affirms the company’s commitment to develop luxurious properties that provide quality leasing and property management services.

Alfardan Properties - Oman offers the same level of high-quality product and service it had developed in its domestic market with a distinct blend of unique architecture and design, and state-of-the-art amenities.

The first project built was a mixed-use development called Finaa Alfardan, situated at the bustling area of Al Azaibah. Inspired from Oman’s cultural identity and distinct architectural flavour, Finaa Alfardan stands out as one of the most attractive residential and business destinations in Oman.

Another outstanding project by Alfardan Properties, situated in the Ghala Heights area of Muscat, is Alfardan Heights. It is a mixed-use property of residences and executive offices featuring more than 150 luxury apartments and over 17,000 square metres of premium office space, retail units, and hospitality services.

Alfardan Heights is easily accessible from all parts of the city, lying halfway between Muscat International Airport and Sultan Qaboos Grand Mosque. Residents benefit from high-class amenities, including a fully-equipped and a gym, temperature-controlled pools, kids’ play areas, a variety of high-end retail stores.

To ensure clients enjoy a distinctive lifestyle, Alfardan Properties has dedicated highly-trained client care personnel to assist tenants with a variety of services ranging from the property selection process to, day-to-day operations.

This commitment serves as the primary objective in the delivery of a full range of value-added services to its clients.

Alfardan Exchange - Qatar

Established in 1971, Alfardan Exchange aims to serve the needs of the region's millions of expatriates and thriving foreign businesses investing in Qatar and the Middle East.

Alfardan Exchange has evolved to become the premier exchange house in Qatar, owing to the introduction of a wide range of financial services with faster and more cost-effective options than those offered by conventional banks, and by offering the best rates and competitive margins.

Alfardan Investment - Qatar

In 1996, Alfardan Investment Company was launched in association with the Qatar Stock Exchange (QSE) to manage the portfolio of Alfardan Group's local shares, as well as specialise in the trading of Alfardan stocks and financial instruments.

The company has been able to add considerable value to the transactions of Alfardan businesses, set high standards for the Group's portfolio, and will continue to play an instrumental role in the expansion of the Group's portfolio and scope of businesses.

Following its long and successful history in the luxury sector, Alfardan Group established the Automotive division as an extension of their diverse businesses in 1996 and becamehome to major automotive companies locally and internationally.



Leading automobile agency Premium Motors, is the official importer of Audi and Volkswagen in Oman. A world class automotive centre, Premium Motors took over the dealership of the global brands on 1st December 2020. Spread across three floors in Alfardan Heights in Ghala, Premium Motor's state-of-the-art showroom stands on a 10,000 square-meter area. A bespoke, modern facility, the Premium Motors showroom has been designed according to global standards focusing on customer experience and journey. Its aftersales facility is backed by a full complement of trained staff and diagnostic technologies. The focus and vision of the dealer is to provide the finest service to Audi and Volkswagen customers in Oman. Premium Motor's ultramodern showroom and service facilities, plus their highly trained staff will benefit the brands and the dealer foresees an exciting future with remarkable new models expected to arrive in the coming years.

Alfardan Motors is the official importer of Ferrari and Maserati in Oman. Since opening its first showroom in 2012, Alfardan Motors has provided the highest levels of service to the growing number of luxury car and motorcycle clients in Oman.

The Ferrari and Maserati showroom has been designed in accordance with the exacting specifications and standards of the Italian sports car manufacturers in terms of Corporate Identity, authenticity, professionalism and luxury.

Sports car enthusiasts of the two Italian brands can custom-fit their dream car in the comfort of the showroom located in Finaa Alazaibah.
